Forge Intelligence is the platform I founded, built, and manage: the intelligence layer behind modern marketing. Drop a URL and Forge reads the brand the way a strategist would (voice, audience, competitive gaps) and returns an intelligence brief in under 10 minutes, free. From there, an 8-agent pipeline with persistent memory turns that worldview into content engineered to be found, and cited, by AI engines.

## The work

I own the platform end to end: product, design, engineering, and go-to-market, bootstrapped from Portland since 2025.

The heart of the system is the Brain, a per-brand competitive worldview that is versioned like code and kept fresh with every analysis. Around it the pipeline runs in sequence: the GEO Strategist claims untaken topical territory, Brand Intelligence maps the landscape, the Authenticity Enricher layers in E-E-A-T signals, and dedicated generators handle content, campaigns, social, video, and ads. Everything passes a compliance gate before the publishing queue ships it with tracking attached, and performance data feeds back into the Brain so the next cycle starts smarter.

## Where it stands

Live, bootstrapped, and compounding. Forge is built for mid-market B2B teams running content operations that compete with teams 10 times their size, and it holds itself to a specific bar: surface the insight a brand strategist would charge $50,000 and 6 weeks to find, continuously. It is not an agency and it does not automate busywork. It is the intelligence layer the marketing operation never had.
