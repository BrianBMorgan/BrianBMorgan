Meridian's science is careful, so their site had to be. The brief asked for credibility without coldness — a marketing site that reads like a journal, not a pitch deck.

## The work

Typography carries the whole thing: a two-family system, hairline rules, and long measure. No hero video, no parallax. The data visualizations are the only color on most pages, which is exactly where the eye should go.

The build is a static Next.js site — every page prerendered, images optimized at build time, zero client-side data fetching. It scores 100 across Lighthouse categories and costs almost nothing to host.

## What endured

- The content team publishes without touching code; everything is Markdown files in the repo.
- 2 years on, the site has survived a rebrand refresh with token changes only — no layout rework.
